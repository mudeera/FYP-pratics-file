ADMISSION POLICY FOR FA/FSC

  1. Admission in 1st year will be given on the basis on the seats available in each subject based on merit.

  2. Admission will be open only once in an academic session, for which college academic committee will announce the admission date.No applicant will e entertained after the due date

  3. the students who have obtained at least 60% marks in SSC-I will be eligible to apply for science (ICS, Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical and Economics group).
    THe studebts who have obtained at least 50% marks in SSC-I will be eligible to apply for Humanities group.

  4. The applicants must tick their priority of subject combination in admission form.

  5. 10 seats are reserved fro other districts(e.g Kharmang, Shigar, Ghanche).

  6. Admission to 2nd year will be given on the basis of performance in 1st year exams conducted by board.

    1. Students who fail in more than three subjects will not be promoted to 2nd year.

    2. Students who fail in 4 subjects will be struck off from the college.

    3. Re-admission is not allowed in case of failure.
